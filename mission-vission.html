Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

HandyFix is dedicated to empowering skilled individuals in Ilocos Norte by connecting them with people who need their services, creating economic opportunities and building stronger communities.

Our Mission

To create a platform that connects skilled individuals with people who need their services, providing economic opportunities for those with valuable skills but without formal employment.

We aim to empower local talent, reduce unemployment, and ensure that every household in Ilocos Norte has access to reliable, skilled service providers for their home maintenance and improvement needs.

Our Vision

To build a thriving ecosystem where skilled individuals can find consistent work opportunities and financial stability, while customers receive high-quality, trustworthy home services.

We envision a future where HandyFix becomes the go-to platform for connecting service providers and customers across the Philippines, creating sustainable livelihoods and stronger communities.

How HandyFix Makes a Difference

For Skilled Workers

  • Economic Opportunity

    Provides a platform for skilled individuals without formal employment to find work and earn a stable income using their existing skills.

  • Flexible Work

    Allows service providers to choose their own hours, set their own rates, and work as much or as little as they want.

  • Skill Recognition

    Values practical skills and experience over formal qualifications, giving opportunities to those with real-world expertise.

  • Business Growth

    Helps skilled individuals build a reputation, expand their client base, and potentially grow into small business owners.

For Customers

  • Reliable Service

    Access to vetted, skilled service providers with verified reviews and ratings from other customers.

  • Convenience

    Easy booking process, transparent pricing, and the ability to find the right person for any home service need.

  • Supporting Local

    Opportunity to support local skilled workers in your community rather than large corporations.

  • Quality Assurance

    Service guarantees and customer support to ensure satisfaction with every job completed.
