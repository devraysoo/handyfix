Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using HandyFix for your home repair and maintenance needs.

How much does it cost to use HandyFix?

HandyFix is free to use for customers. You only pay for the services provided by the service providers. Service providers pay a small commission on completed jobs.

How are service providers vetted?

All service providers undergo background checks, identity verification, and skill assessment before being approved to join our platform. We also monitor reviews and ratings to ensure quality service.

What if I'm not satisfied with the service?

We offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with the service provided, contact our customer support team within 48 hours, and we'll work to resolve the issue.

How do I pay for services?

After the service provider completes the job to your satisfaction, you pay them directly in person. Payment methods are agreed upon between you and the provider, such as cash or mobile payment apps.

What areas do you serve?

Currently, HandyFix operates throughout Ilocos Norte, including Laoag City, Batac, San Nicolas, Dingras, Paoay, and surrounding areas. We're continuously expanding to new locations.

How quickly can I get service?

Service availability depends on provider schedules. Many jobs can be scheduled within 24-48 hours, and we offer emergency services for urgent repairs with faster response times.
